Location: {{ campaign.location }}

Jan Suraksha Insurance Company is organizing this campaign to enhance public awareness of affordable health and safety insurance plans. The campaign aims to educate communities on the importance of insurance coverage, provide information on policy options, and offer personalized consultations. The initiative will also focus on reaching vulnerable groups who may not have easy access to health services. Through targeted workshops and digital resources, we aim to break down barriers to understanding and utilizing health insurance. This campaign will run over a series of weeks, visiting various locations in the region. Local representatives will be available to answer any questions and provide guidance tailored to individual needs. Additionally, we will offer incentives for new policy sign-ups during the campaign. Community leaders will also be invited to speak at certain events to encourage engagement and highlight the importance of securing health coverage.
